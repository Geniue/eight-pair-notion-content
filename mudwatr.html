Step 3. Execute

In the previous tutorial I posted on my socials, I showed how we can get the product for $6.50 per unit from Supliful with our logo/branding already on the packaging.

Order one unit to each of your chosen inquiries.

Tell them to create a video every single day copying the inspiration videos we collected in Step 1. to gain traction.

Keynote - Not everyone will agree to this deal so keep searching for those who do agree if you want to do this for near zero upfront cost. If youre willing to invest $1-2 thousand it will just speed up the process by getting people paid on a 1-2 hundred dollar per week retainer to do this for you.

Now they must upload all the videos into a google drive folder so that you can post all the videos on your Main Profiles on IG reels, TikTok, YT Shorts, Snapchat Spotlight. After repeating this cycle for a week or 2, you should be able to spot some of your videos doing better than others.
