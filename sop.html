SOP for Creator Partner Outreach



Objective



The objective of this Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) is to provide a step-by-step guide for reaching out to up and coming TikTok cooking influencers and collaborating with them to build an e-commerce recipe subscription business off of their audience for partial royalties.



Steps


  1. Identify potential TikTok cooking influencers : Research and identify TikTok cooking influencers with a growing audience and engagement. Look for influencers who have a unique cooking style and have the potential to stand out in the market that also are NOT selling anything yet or just promoting other companies. They would rather own majority in their own company that we build for them.

  2. Research their audience : After identifying potential influencers, research their audience to understand their demographics, interests, and preferences. This will help in developing a suitable subscription model and content for the influencer's audience.

  3. Prepare a partnership proposal: Develop a partnership proposal that outlines the benefits of collaborating with your e-commerce recipe subscription business. The proposal should include:

    • An introduction to your business and its goals

    • A clear explanation of how the partnership will work, including details about the revenue-sharing model and the subscription plan.

    • A marketing strategy that outlines how you plan to promote the subscription plan to the influencer's audience.

    • A value proposition that outlines why the influencer should collaborate with your business and the benefits they will receive.

    • A timeline for the partnership, including when the subscription plan will launch and how long the partnership will last.

    • A call to action that encourages the influencer to respond positively to the proposal and express their interest in collaborating.

      1. Reach out to the influencers: Reach out to the identified influencers through their TikTok account, email, or any other preferred communication channel. Introduce your business and the partnership proposal, and emphasize the potential benefits for both parties.

      2. Negotiate and finalize the terms: Negotiate the terms of the partnership with the influencers, including the revenue-sharing model, the subscription plan, and the marketing strategy. Ensure that all parties are in agreement before finalizing the terms.

      3. Develop the subscription plan and content: Work with the influencers to develop the subscription plan and content that aligns with their audience's interests and preferences. Ensure that the subscription plan is affordable and offers value for money.

      4. Launch and market the subscription plan: Launch the subscription plan and market it through the influencers' TikTok account and other social media channels. Monitor the performance of the subscription plan and make necessary adjustments to improve its success.
