Older Sibling Method


When it comes to purchasing products, people tend to be influenced by various factors, and one of them is the influence of someone with status. This phenomenon is commonly known as the "older sibling method" and has been observed in many consumer behaviors.

The older sibling method is based on the idea that individuals tend to look up to someone who is older, wiser, and more experienced than them. In the context of purchasing products, this could be an older sibling, a parent, a friend, or anyone with whom the person has a close relationship and trusts their opinion.
