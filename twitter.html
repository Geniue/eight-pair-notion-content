Twitter UGC Strategy


Twitter is a great platform to find user-generated content (UGC) creators. By using a software stack including Twitter, Hypefury, Typeform, and relevant hashtags, you can easily find and recruit UGC creators for your brand or business. This is fully automated, takes minutes, and is a crutial step to creating a content flywheel that will produce thousands of dollars a day. ##



Step 1: Create a new Twitter Account

Create a twitter account that appears to be an account that helps provide value and jobs for UGC creators.


Ex. @ugcdaily



Step 2: Follow and engage with UGC profiles


Look up #UGC in the search bar and then follow 50-100 UGC creators.
Like, comment and engage in some of their posts.
This will tigger twitters algorithm to recognize you are interested in UGC content so it will reccomend UGC creators on you “For You Page” along with alos putting your future tweets on theirs.



Step 3: Create a HypeFury Account


HypeFury is a twitter management software that does all kinds of cool stuff like providing automation tools, scheduling and more…
In this particular case we will be creating our tweet on Hypefury and using the “Auto DM” feature to autmatically send out Application forms to our UGC Creator inquirees.



Login to HypeFury using your twitter Login.



Step 4: Create and Post your Tweet with Hypefury



Follow this Template:

#UGC
creator for a $1,000-$5,000/month brand deal.

Like, RT, Comment and DM if you’re interested or know someone who would be a good fit!
#UGCcreator
