If you have an e-commerce supplement brand and you're looking to scale it, one effective strategy is to leverage micro influencers in the personal development and health & wellness niches. Here's a step-by-step process on how to do it:



  • Identify micro influencers who align with your brand values and target audience. Look for influencers with 10,000-50,000 followers who have high engagement rates (likes, comments, shares). You can use tools like BuzzSumo, HypeAuditor, Collabstr and Upfluence to find potential influencers. I suggest just doing manual research on TikTok, IG reels and YT Shorts

  • Reach out to the influencers and offer them an affiliate commission for promoting your products. This means they'll earn a percentage of every sale made through their unique referral link. Set a commission rate that's fair for both parties, typically between 10-30%.
  • Provide the influencers with high-quality product images, descriptions, and content they can use to promote your products. Make it easy for them to promote your brand by giving them everything they need. Or just send them the products so they can use it themselves in their content.

  • Once the influencers start promoting your products, use Facebook Ads and TikTok Ads to retarget the cold audience that's been exposed to your brand through the influencer's promotion. This will help you convert those potential customers into actual customers.

  • Analyze the results and adjust your strategy accordingly. Monitor your sales, engagement, and ROI to determine whether your influencer campaign is effective. If it's not, tweak your approach until you find what works best for your brand.

By following this process, you can effectively scale your e-commerce supplement brand using micro influencers in the personal development and health & wellness niches. Good luck!
