This tool allows you to look inside any E-commerce business and see how much revenue they are producing in real time. Ive tested this tool on my own stores and it is about 85% accurate which is good enoguh for us to know if what the business is doing is a good opportunity to hop on.

5. Remove.bg
Remove.bg is a tool that allows you to remove the background from any image. This is useful for creating product images with a transparent background, which can be more visually appealing and versatile. The tool uses artificial intelligence to automatically detect the subject of the image and remove the background, saving you time and effort in Photoshop or other image editing software.



6. Pexels.com
Pexels.com is a website that provides free stock photos and videos that can be used for personal or commercial projects. The images and videos are high-quality and can be downloaded and used without attribution. Pexels has a large collection of photos and videos across a variety of themes, making it a good resource for finding media for your websites.
