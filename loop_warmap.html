Use the Custom Audience called: “IG Profile Engagers”


To create a Custom Audience on Facebook Ads, follow these steps:


  • Go to your Facebook Ads Manager.
  • Click on the hamburger menu in the upper left corner and select "Audiences".
  • Click on the blue "Create Audience" button and select "Custom Audience".
  • Choose the source of your audience, such as customer file, website traffic, or app activity.
  • Follow the prompts to set up your audience, such as uploading a CSV file or installing a Facebook pixel on your website.
  • Once you've created your Custom Audience, you can use it to target your ads to specific people on Facebook.


To create a Retargeting Facebook Ads CBO Campaign, follow these steps:


  • Go to your Facebook Ads Manager.
  • Click on the "Create" button to create a new campaign.
  • Select "Conversions" as your campaign objective and give your campaign a name.
  • Choose your target audience using the Custom Audience you created earlier called "IG Profile Engagers".
  • In the "Placements" section, select "Automatic Placements".
  • In the "Budget & Schedule" section, select "Campaign Budget Optimization".
  • Set your budget and schedule for the campaign.
  • In the "Ad Set" section, choose your Facebook Page and Instagram account for the ad.
  • Create your ad by choosing the ad format, images or videos, and ad copy.
  • Review your campaign details and click "Publish".

Your Retargeting Facebook Ads CBO Campaign is now created and will target people who engaged with your IG profile.
