Adoption Method



Objective


The objective of this SOP is to outline the process of adopting micro fitness influencers on TikTok who are on the rise and generate sales and brand awareness through their personalized code.


Procedure


Identify potential influencers

  1. Conduct research to identify micro fitness influencers on TikTok who are on the rise and have an engaged audience.

  2. Ensure that the influencers align with our brand values and target audience.


Reach out to potential influencers


  1. Draft an outreach message introducing our brand and the potential collaboration.

  2. Offer the influencers a personalized code to share with their followers, which would provide a discount on our products and generate a commission for the influencer.

  3. Set the commission rate at 20%-30% of the sales generated through their code.
  4. Negotiate the terms of the collaboration with the influencers.


Monitor the collaboration


  1. Track the sales generated through each influencer's personalized code.
  2. Analyze the engagement and reach of each influencer's posts.
  3. Adjust the commission rate or collaboration terms if necessary.


Conclusion



Adopting micro fitness influencers on TikTok who are on the rise is a potential marketing strategy that can increase brand awareness and drive sales. By following this SOP, we can ensure a successful collaboration with these influencers.
