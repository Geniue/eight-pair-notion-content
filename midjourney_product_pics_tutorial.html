Midjourney Product Pics tutorial



Uploading Product Images on Mindjourney


  1. Navigate to Mindjourney and log in to your account.

  2. Click on the "Products" tab in the top menu bar.

  3. Click on the "Add Product" button to create a new product listing.

  4. Fill out the product details, such as the name, description, and price.

  5. Scroll down to the "Images" section.

  6. Click on the "Add Image" button to upload your product image(s).

  7. Select the image file from your computer and click "Open."

  8. Repeat steps 6-7 to add additional images if desired.



Re-creating Product Images with Your Logo


  1. Open your preferred image editing software.

  2. Create a new project with the desired dimensions for your product image.
  3. Import the product image from Mindjourney into your project.

  4. Add your logo to the image in a prominent location. Make sure it does not obscure any important details of the product.

  5. Adjust the size and placement of the logo as necessary.

  6. Save the edited image as a new file.

  7. Return to Mindjourney and navigate to the product listing you wish to update.

  8. Click on the "Edit" button.

  9. Scroll down to the "Images" section.

  10. Click on the "Add Image" button to upload your edited image.

  11. Select the image file from your computer and click "Open."

  12. Delete any old images that you are replacing with the new image.

  13. Click on the "Save" button to update the product listing with your new image.

By following these steps, you can easily upload product images to Mindjourney and enhance them with your own branding.
