Standard Operating Procedure: Launching a Google Brand Search Campaign


Objective:


The objective of this SOP is to provide step-by-step instructions for launching a Google Brand Search Campaign with the objective of driving more sales to your website.


Steps:

  • Start by logging in to your Google Ads account.
  • Click on the "+" button to create a new campaign.
  • Select "Brand Awareness and Reach" as your campaign goal.ulu
  • Choose "Search" as your campaign type.
  • In the "Campaign Name" field, enter a name that clearly identifies the campaign's objective.
  • In the "Locations" section, select the geographic location where you want your ads to appear.
  • In the "Budget" section, set a daily budget that you are comfortable with. This is the maximum amount that you are willing to pay per day for your ads to appear.
  • In the "Bidding" section, select "Manual CPC" as your bidding strategy. This will allow you to set your maximum cost-per-click bid for each keyword manually.
  • In the "Ad Extensions" section, select the ad extensions that you want to use for your campaign.
  • In the "Audiences" section, select your target audience.
  • In the "Keywords" section, enter the keywords that you want to target. These should be relevant to your product and should be selected based on their search volume and relevance to your product.
  • In the "Ad Groups" section, create ad groups for each variant of your product.
  • In the "Ads" section, create ads for each ad group. These ads should include the product's unique selling points and should encourage users to click through to your website.
  • Once you have completed all of these steps, click "Save and Continue" to launch your campaign.

Conclusion:


By following this SOP, you should be able to launch a Google Brand Search Campaign that drives more sales to your website. Remember to monitor your campaign's performance regularly and adjust your keywords, ads, and bidding strategy as needed to achieve your desired results.
