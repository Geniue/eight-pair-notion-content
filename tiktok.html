Step 1. Create new campaign in your ads manager and keep this campaign only for this method. Label it “King of the hill”



Step 2. Initial Testing


  1. Come up with as many creative concepts as possible. Try to stick top videos or photos and have separate campaigns for each media type.

    a. Minimum 10 creatives concepts.


  2. Create 15 identical adsets or adgroups targeting wise, optimizing for VC (View Content). 10 of your ads and 5 of your competitors top ads. Your testing against your own ideas and competitors ideas.


    a. The goal is to get eyes on your ads as fast as possible and as cheap as possible.


    i. Usually takes less than $20 per round of testing and as little as an hour.
    ii. You can set a rule to turn off after 1000 impressions per adset.


    b. Ad each seperate ad creative concept to each seperate adset


    i. this ensures that each audience is as similar as possible and spend is equal. You only want to test creatives here. c. Next you will take your top 5 ads that perform here.


    c. Next you will take your top 5 ads that perform here.


    i. Only 5 is necessary to save time and also becuase thats usually the sweet spot with facebook or tiktok amount of ads wise.
    ii. Also you will look at highest CTR to choose the top 5.


    d. Next is Dessection
    i. Dissect your ad into the following.

    1. Thumbnail
    2. First 2-3 seconds (Hook)
    3. Body (middle section of ad) usually explaining the product in depth.
    4. CTA at the end
    5. Song
    6. Length of video


    e. Now re-do this whole process for each dissection against your competitors original ads.


    f. Through this process you are looking for getting the highest CTR, and (Quality Ranking, Engagement Ranking, Conversion Rate Ranking) all above average.


  3. LAST TEST


    a. Now that you’ve narrowed down on the best possible variations of ads for your market, you need to test them optimizing for Conversions and see if they work for the goal audience.
    b. (Quality Ranking, Engagement Ranking, Conversion Rate Ranking) all above average.
    c. Test with 5-10 adsets in a new ABO campaign


  4. If the test goes well, throw it all into a High budget CBO broad and watch the magic happen.

  5. If the test doesnt workout, the competitors still have better creatives and you need to keep testing.
