ALS Ice Bucket Challenge - Strategy



The ALS Ice Bucket Challenge went viral due to several factors. First, it was a unique and engaging way to raise awareness and funds for ALS research. The challenge involved individuals dumping a bucket of ice water over their head and challenging others to do the same or donate to ALS research. This created a fun and viral social media trend that spread quickly.


Second, the challenge was easy to participate in and share with others. Participants could easily record and share videos of themselves completing the challenge on social media platforms such as Facebook and Twitter.


Finally, the challenge was supported by many celebrities and public figures, which helped to increase its visibility and credibility. This led to widespread media coverage and further contributed to the challenge's success.


Overall, the ALS Ice Bucket Challenge's virality can be attributed to its unique and engaging nature, ease of participation and sharing, and support from influential individuals.
